About the Agency

Kelowna's luxury vacation rental specialists

We manage the top one percent of properties in the Okanagan Valley and host VIPs and public figures with the utmost discretion. We are based out of beautiful Kelowna, BC Canada.


We work with a select group of remote real estate investors to take high-profile properties and turn them into lucrative businesses. We act with the same care and attention to detail that we give our personal investment properties.


The vacation rental industry is based on relationships and we attract high-net-worth individuals as both clients and guests (who eventually become clients).


We believe whether you partner with us, stay with us, or work for us we will leave you with a lasting impression of excellence.

About Deana

Introducing Deana Steele

Deana love’s to travel but she’s never found a better place to live and has called Kelowna home for over 30 years. While working remotely, she always kept her home fully furnished and offered it as an executive rental so she could easily return. The high rents collected supported her entrepreneurial endeavours.


Deana’s a driven entrepreneur, always looking for opportunities. She says, “There’s nothing more empowering than creating something out of nothing.”


After her personal success with her property on Airbnb, Keys to Kelowna started naturally in 2013. Airbnb pursued Deana to help remote real estate investors by managing their properties like they were her own. In her first year, the portfolio grew from 1 to 23 listings.


She now focuses on hand-selecting great clients, the best properties and confirming reservations for VIPs, while her team takes care of day-to-day tasks.


Deana is passionate about health and longevity which is another reason she calls Kelowna home. Kelowna is rich in year-round outdoor amenities, clean air and water. In her downtime, you will find Deana hiking, biking, taking relaxing walks and squeezing in a few rounds of golf with her long-time partner Ryan.


She makes the added effort to manage stress because there is nothing more important than a healthy, balanced lifestyle…and a sleep score of 99/100.


She is also a biohacker with her own “Longevity Lab” equipped with an infrared sauna, full-body infrared & red light panels, compression recovery suit and more.


Deana wants everyone to experience the beauty of Kelowna and the power of building a diverse real estate investment portfolio.



Find her on Instagram. She loves to connect and share everything about the business.
